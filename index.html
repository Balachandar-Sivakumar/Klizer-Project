How much does Klizer Cost?
Klizer’s basic plan starts from $40,000 for Adobe open source, Adobe Commerce, Shopify, and BigCommerce platforms. Klizer offers flexible pricing options to accommodate various project needs. For Fixed Bid/Project-based arrangements, we provide a complimentary 30-day support period after the project’s launch, exclusively for bug fixes. No new features will be developed during this period. Afterward, clients have the option to acquire additional support through a Time and Material (T&M) model. They can opt for a minimum of 100 hours of support, valid for at least 60 days. Moreover, we offer retainer engagements tailored to the client’s needs, providing ongoing support where developers will be fully retained or dedicatedly working on their projects. The fees for our services are based on hourly rates, which are set at $95/hour. For retainer engagements, pricing is customized based on specific requirements. We encourage interested clients to schedule a call with us to discuss their needs further, after which we can provide tailored pricing details.
